Job Description/Duties - 
- National Economic Transformation and 5Es Project aims to stimulate sustainable economic development while promoting equity and empowerment across various sectors. The Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E) Department under the Support Unit is dedicated to assessing project performance, ensuring accountability, and informing stakeholders about progress. Monitoring and Evaluation Officer will be responsible for designing and implementing M&E frameworks, tools, and processes that effectively track project outputs, outcomes, and impact. This role is essential for providing evidence-based insights to guide decision-making and enhance project performance. 
- Design comprehensive monitoring and evaluation frameworks aligned with project goals and industry best practices. 
- Establish key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics for assessing project progress and impact. 
- Develop and implement data collection methodologies, ensuring the reliability and validity of data. 
- Conduct quantitative and qualitative data analysis to assess project performance and outcomes. 
- Prepare regular monitoring reports, evaluation studies, and presentations that summarize findings and provide actionable insights for stakeholders. 
- Ensure timely and accurate reporting to project management and stakeholders as per agreed timelines. Conduct training sessions and workshops for project staff and partners on M&E concepts, tools, and practices. 
-  Foster a culture of learning within the project team by encouraging the use of M&E findings for continuous improvement. 
- Work closely with project teams and stakeholders to ensure integration of M&E activities into project planning and implementation. 
- Facilitate stakeholder engagement in the M&E process, ensuring their perspectives are reflected in evaluations. 
- Identify areas for improvement in project implementation and recommend strategies based on M&E findings. 
-  Stay updated on M&E trends and best practices to enhance project effectiveness. 
-  Any other task assigned by Supervisor/Head of Support Unit
